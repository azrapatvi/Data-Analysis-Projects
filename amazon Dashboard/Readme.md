### **Overview**
An analytical Power BI dashboard for **Amazon sales data**, designed to visualize key metrics like total orders, cancelled orders, and category-wise sales.

### **Dashboard Highlights**
- **Cards & KPI visuals:** Total Sales, Orders, and Cancellations  
- **Donut & Funnel Charts:** Order breakdowns and conversion flows  
- **Ribbon Chart:** Category performance over time  
- **Line Charts:** Trends in sales by payment method and category  

### **Key Learnings**
- Strengthened understanding of **ranking visuals** (Ribbon Chart).  
- Explored **customer and payment insights**.  
- Practiced **clean and interactive dashboard design**.
